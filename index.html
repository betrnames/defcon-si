Methodology

  • SRC  Open sources only: news wires, government releases, flight/maritime trackers, official social channels, public research.
  • EST  The DEFCON figure is an estimate inferred from observable posture (deployments, alerts, rhetoric, exercises). The real level is classified and frequently differs by command.
  • SI   SI Readiness is a machine-scored composite (0–5) weighing signal volume, severity, corroboration, and rate of change against a rolling baseline.
  • REV  Assessments are revised as signals are corroborated or retracted. Confidence tags on each card reflect sourcing quality, not certainty of outcome.
